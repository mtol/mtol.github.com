About

Dr. Linden Mackenzie-Tolhurst MCMBA MAACMA is a Melbourne-based practitioner of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), qualified in both Acupuncture and Chinese Herbal Medicine. Linden completed a Bachelor of Health Science (Traditional Chinese Medicine) at the Southern School of Natural Therapies, and is currently a Master of Health Science (TCM) candidate at the University of Western Sydney.

Linden is also registered with the Chinese Medicine Board of Australia (CMBA), the gold standard for Chinese medicine practice in Australia as well as a member of the Australian Acupuncture and Chinese Medicine Association (AACMA), the premier professional association for practitioners of Chinese medicine.
