Linden Mackenzie-Tolhurst

Traditional Chinese herbal medicine and acupuncture practitioner based in Melbourne, Australia. Offerring treatments for a broad range of conditions to assist in restoring balance within your body and achieving better health, drug-free.

Services

Linden offers acupuncture with associated ancillary techniques (cupping, moxa, gua sha), Chinese herbal medicine and dietary therapy to provide an overall approach to health.

The clinic is accessible to those in the Melbourne area.

Treatable Ailments

Chinese medicine is effective in the treatment of many conditions, including: musculoskeletal disorders, acute and soft tissues sports injuries, neurological disorders, skin disorders, gastrointestinal tract (GIT) disorders, weight loss, infertility, stress and related disorders, respiratory and seasonal disorders, and women's health.
